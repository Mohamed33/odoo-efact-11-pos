Odoo 11 Point of Sale Extension

Allows user to see available stock of product from POS interface and restrict sale when product out of stock.


Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo community edition but not tested in Enterprise version. And we are supporting Ubuntu OS and not Windows OS.

Features

  • Display product stock in POS
  • Restrict Product selling if not in stock but can sell item from other stock locations.
  • Maintain product stock in POS cart
