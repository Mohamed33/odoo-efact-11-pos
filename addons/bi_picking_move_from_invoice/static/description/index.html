Stock Move And Picking From Invoice

Update stock automatically when validate Invoice.

Normally we can create Picking and Stock move when we confirm sale order in odoo but default odoo does not provide such functionality to create stock moves and picking directly from invoice.

In major cases with some businesses provide direct invoices to their customers so they cannot maintain stock. It can save your time if stock moves and picking get created automatically with invoices.

To make that use-cases simple we have added this module to make work fast and simple.

Click here for the video tutorial

key features:

  • Have option to create stock picking or stock move from the invoice.
  • Able to create picking from the customer invoice as well as from vendor bills.
  • Auto selected picking type. When you make picking/move from the customer invoice it will generated delivery order/moves and when you make picking/move from the vendor bills its generated incoming shipments(moves).

How to use

Stock Move And Picking From Invoice

There is two options in Settings of Invoicing, If we choose 'Create Stock Picking From Invoice'


In new invoice there is a button of picking right side of corner.


After clicking on the Validate button, It will create a picking of particular invoice directly.



Related Picking of particular invoice.


With the selection of second option 'Create Stock Move From Invoice'.


In invoice we have another button of stock move.


After clicking on the Validate button, It will create moves of particular invoice directly.
